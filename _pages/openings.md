### How to apply
Interested candidates may email Dr. Haipeng Yu (email address) your CV, transcripts, and a brief statement of research interest and experience with the subject line **"Application to AAI Lab"**. Candidates with skills/experiences in statistical modeling, machine learning, computer vision, and computer programming (C/C++, Java, Julia, Python, or R) are encouraged to apply.
